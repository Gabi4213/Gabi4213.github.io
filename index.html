About Me

I am 19 years old currently studying Computer Games Programming while being a game developer/content creator on YouTube. I have been working mainly with Unity and C# for over a year now creating my own games, projects and other awesome things! I am currently working on my personal project an FPS story adventure game that I am developing solo in Unity. I update my audience with monthly devlog videos on my YouTube. In the mean time I am working on other various videos for my YouTube channel while learning C++ and further programming at Staffordshire University. Over the past 4 years of game development I have participated in quite a few game jams such as GMTK, Ludum Dare 46, The BlackThornProd Game Jam and more. I currently upload most of my work to Itch.io. But many of my projects are not available there but can be found on my YouTube. I also love creating art and graphic design related things in Adobe Photoshop & Illustrator. Not to mention I also frequently work in Adobe Premier Pro & After Effects where I create and edit videos for my channel.
